FuelTrack Pro helps you log refuels, track spending, and measure fuel efficiency across one or many vehicles — entirely on your device.

Features:
• Multi-vehicle garage with registration, fuel type, and photos
• Smart refuel entry (enter any two of quantity, price, or total)
• Dashboard and analytics with charts and period filters
• PDF fuel reports with registration plates, stations, and refuel history
• Local JSON backup and restore
• Optional Google Drive sync (uses Google services when enabled)
• Fuel cards, service reminders, and Material 3 light/dark themes

No account is required for core tracking. Your data stays in local SQLite unless you export or back up.
