Privacy Policy

FuelTrack Pro
Effective date: June 27, 2026
Contact: wealth-journal@proton.me

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the developer ("I", "me", or "the developer") handles information in connection with the FuelTrack Pro mobile application ("the App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

FuelTrack Pro is a local-first fuel and vehicle expense tracker. Your refuels, vehicles, fuel cards, service reminders, and related settings are stored on your device and are not sent to a FuelTrack server, because no such server exists. Data may leave your device only in the ways described below (for example optional cloud backup or exports you explicitly start).

2. Information stored on your device

2.1 Data you enter

Information you add in the App — vehicles, refuel entries, odometer readings, fuel card assignments, service schedules, preferences, and similar records — is stored in a SQLite database on your device. It does not leave your device except when you use optional backup or export features, or when the operating system backs up app data according to your device settings.

2.2 No FuelTrack account

The App does not require you to create a FuelTrack account or register an email with me. There is no backend operated by me that stores your fuel or vehicle data. If you use optional Google Drive backup, sign-in is with Google under Google's terms — not a separate FuelTrack login.

2.3 Optional Google Drive sync

If you use Google Drive sync, the App may use Google Sign-In and the Google Drive API to store a JSON backup of your fuel data in a Google-managed app-specific area tied to the Google account you choose. The backup is not passphrase-encrypted — protect your Google account accordingly. I do not receive your Google password or a copy of your data on my servers. Google processes authentication and storage under Google's Privacy Policy. You can turn sync off in the App settings.

3. Exports

You may generate CSV exports from within the App. These files are created on demand and saved or shared only where you direct. Treat exports as sensitive if they contain your data. I never receive a copy of your exports.

4. Data I do not collect (developer-side)

I do not operate analytics, advertising, or a FuelTrack user database. I do not collect or have access to:

  • Your refuel history, vehicle details, or spending data in the clear
  • A separate FuelTrack login or email list from the App
  • First-party crash reporting or behavioural analytics sent to me
  • Advertising IDs or ad SDK data from the App

The App contains no advertising SDKs and shows no advertisements from the developer.

5. Internet access

Network access is used only when a feature needs it — for example optional Google sign-in and Drive backup when you enable it, or opening links you choose (such as this policy or support email). The App does not upload your SQLite database to a developer server.

6. Device permissions

Depending on your device and settings, the App may request permissions such as Internet (for optional backup and links), notifications (for local service reminders if you enable them), and storage or file access to save backups and exports via system file pickers. Permissions are used for the stated features only.

7. Security

Your data is stored locally on your device. Google Drive sync stores a readable JSON backup in your Google account — use a strong Google account password and device lock. No security measure is perfect — you are responsible for your device and any cloud accounts you use.

8. Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a minor has used the App without appropriate consent, contact me at wealth-journal@proton.me.

9. Changes

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. I will update the effective date at the top when I do. Continued use of the App after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
